Anthony Ho - P3 - Documentation

Making the Site

I wanted to created a top down shooter. It was suppose to a game where the player
Sees how many points they can get before they die.

Process

In my proposal, I wanted to the player to move around and collect batteries to
to recharge their suit. However due to time constraints, I was unable to make that. Instead
I choose to make it so that the player is unable to move. They must defend themselves from the
Aliens that are coming towards them. If enough of them gets to the player, it's game over

The characters were drawn by me while the background was photoshoped.Most of the times was spent
trying to figure out how to get the character to spin with the mouse.
