Alien Survivor

You are a survivor in a space station. Aliens are attacking and you need to defend yourself. Collect as many batteries as you can to fuel your gun while avoiding getting eaten.




Genre: Horror

Platform: Desktop web

Story: Abstract

Gameplay: Top down shooter. Your gun will slowly lose batteries as time goes by and as you shoot. You can collect batteries that spawn randomly on the screen to refill.
